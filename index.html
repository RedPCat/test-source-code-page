Enchanted Relics: Change the enchantment glint color on your gear

Relics are found in dig sites when doing archaeology, and they have 16 different colors indicated by their enchantment glint. When combined with an enchanted item, it changes the enchantment glint to the relic's enchantment glint. You can have a sword that glows red, or a pickaxe that glows blue. This would make archaeology more aesthetic and useful.
